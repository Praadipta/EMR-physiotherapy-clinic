
make App for Healthcare Provider

case:
physiotherapy clinic "Sambung Nyowo"

language :
bahasa Indonesia

---------
Functional Requirements :

1. Patient Management
Register new patients (name, DOB, gender, contact, address)
Assign unique patient ID
View and update patient profiles
Search patients by name or ID
View patient visit history

2. Appointment & Scheduling
Create and manage therapy appointments
Assign physiotherapist to each session
View daily and weekly schedules
Update appointment status (scheduled, completed, canceled, no-show)
Appointment in calendar view

3. Clinical Documentation (Physiotherapy-Focused)
Record initial assessment:
Chief complaint
Injury or condition
Affected body part
Pain scale
Range of motion notes
Record therapy session notes:
Treatment provided
Exercises performed
Patient response
Progress notes per session
Simple outcome tracking (improved, unchanged, worsened)

4. Treatment Plan Management
Create treatment plans per patient
Define goals and number of planned sessions
Track treatment status (planned, in-progress, completed)
Update treatment plan as needed

5. Billing & Payments (Basic)
Record therapy service charges
Generate simple invoices
Record payment status (paid, unpaid, partial)
View patient payment history
---
6. User & Role Management
authentication, session management, & logout function
User roles:
Physiotherapist
Admin / Reception

Role-based access:
Therapists access clinical notes
Admin manages appointments and billing

---
7. Reporting
Daily patient visit list
Therapist workload summary
Basic revenue report
---------------
